about me

My name is Emanuel Castillo, I'm a developer, entrepreneur and a Polyglot. I was born in March 5, 1994. One of my hobbies was to do Parkour but now I'm “retired”. Now I dedicate my time to code, help people learn English and study online among other things. I'm a very passionate for languages and online education. My mother language is Spanish because I grew up in Colombia, my second language English and the third one Dutch.


I also can speak a bit of German, French and Portuguese, but just as a hobby… I've created several “businesses”, many of them failed, you can know more about them in my biography. I learnt to code and created izipiziCourse.com, a site to learn English for Spanish-speakers. Later I founded MyLearny.com, an online educational platform for Latin America.
